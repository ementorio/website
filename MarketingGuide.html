Career Guidance - Marketing

High School Preparation (4-5 years)
  • Develop strong communication and writing skills.
  • Study subjects like business, economics, and psychology.
  • Participate in marketing-related extracurricular activities.
Bachelor's Degree (4 years)
  • Pursue a Bachelor's degree in Marketing or a related field.
  • Gain knowledge in digital marketing and analytics.
  • Complete internships in marketing departments.
Internships and Experience (1-2 years)
  • Apply for internships in advertising agencies or marketing firms.
  • Learn about market research and consumer behavior.
  • Build a portfolio of marketing campaigns and projects.
Entry-Level Positions (1-2 years)
  • Apply for marketing coordinator or assistant roles.
  • Work on marketing strategy and content creation.
  • Continue learning about emerging marketing trends.
Professional Development
  • Stay updated with digital marketing tools and platforms.
  • Consider obtaining marketing certifications (e.g., Google Ads, HubSpot).
  • Network with industry professionals and attend marketing conferences.
