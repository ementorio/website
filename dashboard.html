Career Guidance

Marketing

Marketing involves promoting and selling products or services to consumers. It's a dynamic field that requires creativity and analytical skills.

Economics

Economics studies the production and distribution of goods and services. Economists analyze data to make informed decisions in various industries.

Aerospace Engineering

Aerospace engineers design and develop aircraft, spacecraft, and related systems. It's a challenging field that explores the wonders of flight and space exploration.

Design

Designers create visual concepts using computer software or by hand. They communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers.

Programming

Programmers write code to create software programs and applications. It's a field that requires problem-solving and logical thinking.

Healthcare

Healthcare professionals provide medical care to patients. It includes a wide range of careers such as doctors, nurses, and pharmacists.
