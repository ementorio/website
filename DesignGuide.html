Career Guidance - Design

High School Preparation (4-5 years)
  • Develop a strong foundation in art and design.
  • Experiment with various design software and tools.
  • Build a portfolio of creative projects.
Bachelor's Degree (4 years)
  • Pursue a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Industrial Design, or a related field.
  • Master design software and principles.
  • Collaborate on design projects and internships.
Portfolio Building (1-2 years)
  • Create a diverse and impressive design portfolio.
  • Showcase personal and professional projects.
  • Attend design events and exhibitions.
Entry-Level Positions (1-2 years)
  • Apply for junior designer or assistant positions.
  • Work on design projects and client assignments.
  • Build professional relationships in the design industry.
Advanced Roles and Specialization
  • Consider specializing in areas like web design, UX/UI, or branding.
  • Lead design teams and manage projects.
  • Stay updated with design trends and technologies.
